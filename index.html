About Jyotika

Jyotika Kangsia is a professional artist, educator, and creative mentor with over 6 years of experience in sketching, charcoal art, acrylic painting, portrait drawing, and custom artwork creation. She holds B.Sc., M.Sc., and B.Ed. qualifications, combining academic excellence with artistic creativity. Over the years, she has created a diverse portfolio of realistic sketches, charcoal portraits, acrylic paintings, spiritual artwork, and customized commissions. Her mission is to inspire aspiring artists by making art learning simple, structured, and enjoyable. Through Art with Jyotika, she offers professional training programs in sketching, charcoal art, and acrylic painting, helping students build strong artistic foundations while developing confidence in their creative abilities.
